An android anti-theft app.
## Functions ##
### Auto Mode ###
  * Check SIM card
### Tele Mode ###
  * SMS control
### Protecting data ###
  * Wipe SDcard
  * Factory reset
### Find Phone ###
  * Get location infomation
  * Take pictures
